About Punkalasa Atapirikara

Established in 2010, Punkalasa Atapirikara has been serving the Buddhist community in Boralesgamuwa with high-quality religious offerings and temple supplies.

We specialize in traditional Sri Lankan pirikara including flowers, oil lamps, incense, and complete puja sets for temple offerings and home worship.

Our mission is to provide authentic, ethically-sourced Buddhist religious items while preserving traditional craftsmanship.
